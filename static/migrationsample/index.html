Why a Walk-through and Tutorial Site?

We Want to Leverage SharePoint

SharePoint is designed to handle millionsof documents, but in order to allow the platform to do the heavy lifting, we need to delimit Editorial standards and migrate all existing ColdFusion sites to the new platform.

We Want to Empower Content Owners

Nobody understands the content on AAP.org better than the individual content owners, and training content owners is the only way to maximize the potential of this new CMS (content management system).
